Me as a Teacher

Teaching gives me a great sense of passion and I really enjoy seeing my students improve in language skills and they can use the target language to communicate with the people of other countries and learn about their cultures. I've been teaching Persian and English for about 10 years. I'm also a language learner, so I understand all the challenges of learning a second language, and I can help the students to overcome the barriers and become a fluent, confident speaker. My students know me as a patient and creative teacher.

My Lessons & Teaching Style

My teaching style is energetic and effective. Students enjoy learning and feel at ease to communicate with others. I have picked up many tips and strategies that help the students to improve in their speaking through real-life conversations. I'm also a vocabulary and grammar specialist through presentation slides, images, and audio files which help the students to learn easily. My lesson plan is flexible based on the student's needs.
